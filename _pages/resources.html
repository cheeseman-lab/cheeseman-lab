Resources

Plasmids

Our lab has generated numerous plasmids for studying kinetochore and cell division proteins. Plasmids are available through Addgene.

Browse Our Plasmids on Addgene

Vesuvius

An interactive web portal for exploring image-based CRISPR screens of essential genes in human cells. Browse phenotypic data from our microscopy-based genetic screens.

Explore Vesuvius Database

Cell Cycle Knockouts Database

We have generated a collection of inducible CRISPR/Cas9-based knockout human cell lines targeting 209 genes with key roles in cell cycle functions. This resource enables precise studies of cell cycle structures, processes, and checkpoints. The database includes micrographs from analyses of knockout cell lines in HeLa cells.

Explore Cell Cycle Knockouts Database
