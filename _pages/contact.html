Postdoctoral Fellows

We welcome applications from potential postdoctoral fellows. Please email Iain at icheese@wi.mit.edu for potential postdoc opportunities.

Graduate Students

MIT graduate students across all departments can join the lab through a rotation. Please contact cheeseman_admin@wi.mit.edu to learn more about rotation opportunities. External graduate students interested in opportunities should also email us to discuss possibilities for joining the lab.

Undergraduate Students

MIT students can join the lab through the UROP program. Please email cheeseman_admin@wi.mit.edu to discuss potential projects. External undergraduate students interested in research opportunities are encouraged to apply to the MSRP program or to contact us to learn about possible positions.
